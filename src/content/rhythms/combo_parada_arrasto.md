Pre-requisites:
- Parada
- Arrasto

Instructions:

This combo repeats Parada 3x and after a transition we play Arrasto three times until we come back to Parada.

```rhythm
Alfaia:
. R . . | R L R . | R L R . | R L . R | L R . . | . . . . | . . . . | . . . . |
. R . . | R L R . | R L R . | R L . R | L R . . | . . . . | . . . . | . . . . |
. R . . | R L R . | R L R . | R L . R | . L . R | . L . R | . L R . | R L . R | L R . . |
. L R . | . L R . | . L R . | . R . . |
. L R . | . L R . | . L R . | . R . . |
. L R . | . L R . | . L R . |
```
